Order Structures & MOQs

Scale from pilot pallets to full containers. We outline minimums but stay flexible for mixed loads.

Order Type Typical MOQ Notes
Trial Pallet Program 1–2 pallets / 150–250 lamps Best for market testing. Ships LCL or air freight.
Mixed Container (20') 8–12 MT mixed SKUs Combine categories with dedicated pallet maps.
Full Container (40') Up to 24 MT Optimized for distributors and national retailers.

Lead Times & Shipping Terms

  • Production: 3–5 weeks depending on finishing and volume.
  • FOB Karachi or Port Qasim with nominated forwarder handoff.
  • We can support CFR/CIF quotes upon request.
  • Weekly status recaps with photos at key milestones.

Quality & Compliance

  • Dual QC — pre-purchase and pre-shipment.
  • Batch coding, moisture reads, and wiring tests (for lamps).
  • Packaging audits before sealing pallets or cartons.
  • Document archive shared digitally for your compliance teams.

Documentation Set

We export under Salterra Naturals as exporter of record.

  • Commercial Invoice & Packing List.
  • Certificate of Origin (Chamber or FTA as required).
  • HS code declaration for each SKU.
  • Fumigation / Phytosanitary certificates when destination requires.
  • Bill of Lading draft approval + final originals or telex release.
