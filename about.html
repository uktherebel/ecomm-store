Salterra Naturals is a Pakistan-based trading & export company focused on Himalayan salt.

We are not a broker or tourist gift shop. We act as your on-ground sourcing, QC, and logistics partner for shipments leaving Pakistan.

Our Story

Founded to connect serious importers with consistent Himalayan salt supply.

Salterra Naturals launched after years spent supporting overseas spa builders and specialty retailers who struggled with inconsistent supply, poor packaging, and limited communication from ad-hoc exporters. We saw an opportunity to bring Western-style project management to an industry still dominated by informal brokers. Today, our small team operates between Lahore and Karachi, coordinating production with vetted factories in the Khewra region and managing export documentation under our own name.

We keep operations intentionally focused: Himalayan salt lamps, tiles, bulk salt, bath goods, cooking slabs, and carved items. This focus lets us monitor quality deeply, negotiate favorable production slots, and consolidate mixed shipments without confusion. Many of our clients reorder multiple times per year, trusting us to keep SKUs compliant and on schedule.

Communication Style

You work directly with our operations managers — no handoffs to unknown brokers. We respond to enquiries within one business day, keep WhatsApp and email threads organized, and document every decision. Weekly recaps summarize production percentages, QC findings, and ETDs so your team can plan retail launches or spa builds with confidence.

Responsibility & QC

Himalayan salt is fragile and heavy. We manage it accordingly: climate-controlled storage, moisture testing, independent weight checks, and redundant packaging. Before a shipment leaves Lahore, we photograph every pallet, capture seal numbers, and upload documentation to a shared drive. This discipline prevents claims and saves weeks during customs clearance.

Who We Support

Importers, spa and sauna builders, salt room contractors, food-service wholesalers, and Amazon-focused brands rely on us to curate SKUs, meet compliance requirements, and ship on time. Some partners use us as their outsourced product team — we photograph SKUs, build spec sheets, and coordinate packaging tailored to their markets.

Looking Ahead

Our roadmap centers on better digital documentation, on-site QC rooms with live streaming for clients, and additional finished goods that pair Himalayan salt with complementary materials like ceramics and botanicals. The goal stays the same: make Pakistan-based sourcing feel as structured as working with a domestic supplier.
